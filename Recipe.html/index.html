Chocolate Chicken Recipe


Picture reference link

Description about the Recipe:

Saute chicken with "Xocai Healthy Dark Chocolate" and you will have a deliciously sweet and savory healthy meal the whole family will beg for again and again!

Ingredients:

  • 2 whole boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 tsp ground pepper (coarse)
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup "Xocai Activ powder"
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 6 "Xocai Omega Squares" finely chopped
  • 1 "Xocai X Powerhouse Cookie"
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1/4 cup spiced rum

Preparation instructions:

  1. Take chili powder, kosher salt, pepper, and cinnamon and mix it in a bowl with the "Xocai Activ" and flour. Combine well.
  2. Heat saute pan, and place some olive oil in your pan.
  3. Dredge the chicken breast within the flour mixture, lay carefully in the hot saute pan, and brown on both sides for about 3-5 minutes.
  4. Add finely chopped "Xocai Omega Squares," butter, and a slash of spiced rum.
  5. Let the alcohol cook off for about 2-4 minutes, moving the chicken around and mixing the "Xocai Omega Square" and butter together to create the sauce. It is done when the internal temperature is 150 degrees
  6. Let rest ans then coarsely chop the "Xocai X Powerhouse Cookie" and sprinkle on the top of the chicken.
